In a world where ancient ruins whisper forgotten secrets and the horizon hides dangers yet to be named, your journey is about to begin. Legends speak of heroes who rose from nothing, shaped not only by fate, but by the choices they made in their earliest moments. Now, it is your turn to step into that story.

Before you lies an adventure filled with peril, mystery, and glory beyond imagination. Dark forces stir in the shadows, treasures await those bold enough to seek them, and every path you choose will carve your name into the fabric of this world. But every great hero must begin somewhere…

To create your character, you will determine your core statistics through a test of chance and fortune. For each statistic, you will roll two twenty-sided dice (2d20). The system will automatically select the higher result between the two rolls, representing your character’s natural strengths and potential.
